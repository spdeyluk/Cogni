Questions

How long does it take to see changes?

It varies by person and consistency. Most people train a few minutes most days; the point is a sustainable habit, not a single session.

Is this a replacement for therapy or medical care?

No. Cogni is a training and self-tracking tool. If you're dealing with a health concern, talk to a qualified professional.

What if I miss days?

Nothing breaks. Your progress is saved to your account — pick up where you left off whenever you're ready.

Is it suitable for students and younger users?

The exercises are designed to be broadly accessible. Younger users should have a parent or guardian involved.

Adaptive test

Cogni IQ Test

The test adapts to you: each answer picks the next question. Most sessions take 10–25 minutes and finish in 20–35 questions.

  • Find a quiet spot and don't use a calculator or notes.
  • Each question has its own timer — an unanswered question counts as incorrect.
  • You can't go back to earlier questions.
  • Don't refresh or close the app; if it happens, the test resumes where you left off.

Scores are estimates from provisional item data, for personal tracking only — this is not a clinically validated IQ test.
